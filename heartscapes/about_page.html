About Heartscapes

Heartscapes began as a 7-day interactive digital diary where emotions, feelings, and memories take shape through 3D modeling, code, and animation. Inspired by the creator's own journey of self-discovery, Heartscapes provides a space for translating the complexities of emotional experiences into tangible, abstract forms. Heartscapes goes beyond the digital realm by changing daily, reflecting the creator's own emotions.


Like emotions, this site is constantly shifting. Come back daily!
