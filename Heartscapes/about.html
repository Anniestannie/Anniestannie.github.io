About Heartscapes

Heartscapes is a unique digital diary that explores the depths of human emotions through 3D modeling, design, code, and animation. Inspired by the creator's own journey of self-discovery, Heartscapes provides a space for translating the complexities of emotional experiences into tangible, abstract forms. Heartscapes goes beyond the digital realm by organically evolving daily, reflecting the creator's own emotions and creating a unique connection between the physical self and the digital landscape.
